A Supermarket Built on Trust & Freshness

Sunrise Supermarket has been a cornerstone of the local community in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Located conveniently in the neighbourhood, we have grown from a small provision store to a full-fledged supermarket serving hundreds of families every week.

We believe that grocery shopping should be simple, affordable, and reliable. Our shelves are stocked daily with fresh produce, quality packaged goods, dairy products, frozen foods, and household essentials — everything a family needs under one roof.

Our friendly staff, clean store environment, and fair pricing have earned us the trust of the local community. We are proud to be your go-to destination for all daily needs.
